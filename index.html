Crystals Collector

Here are the rules:

*You will be tasked with a random number at the beginning of the game.
*There are four crystals, each with different hidden values.
*Click on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to your total score.
*If you match your total score to the random number, you win!
*If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.
*The values of each crystal will changes with each new game.
Good Luck!
